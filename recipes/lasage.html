Lasagne

This is a delicious lasagne which originates from Italy. It is a delicious meal that is easy to make and can be eaten at any time of the day.

ingredient list

  • 1/2 cup of minced meat
  • 1/2 cup of cheese
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of lasagne sheets
  • 1/2 cup of bechamel sauce

steps

  1. cook the minced meat
  2. cook the bechamel sauce
  3. put the lasagne sheets in a oven dish
  4. put the minced meat, tomato sauce, bechamel sauce and cheese on the lasagne sheets
  5. put another layer of lasagne sheets on top of the meat
  6. repeat the process until you have no more ingredients
  7. put the lasagne in the oven for 30 minutes
  8. enjoy

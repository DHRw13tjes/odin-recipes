Burito

This is a delicious burito which originates from mexico but has a good root from mexico. that is why it is called texmex. It is a delicious meal that is easy to make and can be eaten at any time of the day.

ingredient list

  • 1 tortilla
  • 1/2 cup of rice
  • 1/2 cup of beans
  • 1/2 cup of meat
  • 1/2 cup of cheese
  • 1/2 cup of salsa

steps

  1. cook the rice
  2. cook the beans
  3. cook the meat
  4. put the tortilla on a plate
  5. put the rice, beans, meat, cheese and salsa on the tortilla
  6. roll the tortilla
  7. enjoy
